Northern Indiana

The majority of northern Indiana is farmland. It is beautiful country, and it is generally flatter, but higher elevation than southern Indiana. It is filled with gorgeous countryside and interesting sights.

Southern Indiana

Southern Indiana has some farmland, but the further south you go, the more rugged the country gets. It is also filled with wonderful sights.
